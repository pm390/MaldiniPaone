4.x Scenario x 
Fred asks to his friend Manuel if he wants to join him for dinner this evening. Unfortunately
Fred's car is blocked by a car parked in front of his garage, that car was there several days every month.
Normally Fred working nearby has no problem but his friends lives 30 minutes by car from his home.
John picks up his smartphone and downloads SafeStreets app.
John provides his information to the system , once registered he logs in and sends a notification to authorities.
One hour before leaving home he notices the car has been moved. From that day on the car owner stopped parking there.
4.x+1 Scenario x+1
Today in Milan there are lot of violations and authorities aren't enough to check every violation. The violations not taken care
after some time goes down in the list of assignments of authorities. Agent Carlo is checking the areas between Milano Piola and Milano Lambrate.
Lot of violations are notified by users in those areas. By the time he finishes to check some violations other violations become less 
visible due to the great amount of notification.When a violation is notified for the same licence plate from different users in the same area
those violations become more visible in the list of assignments of authorities. Carlo thanks to this feature is able to first adress
issues affecting more people
4.x+2 Negative Use Scenario 1
Fernand got his car fined after parking in a forbidden area. He has been doing it for years and he notices that people in that area started
using SafeStreets app and thinks the app is the cause of the fine and doesn't relize his behaviour is the cause. He decides to "take revenge"
subscribing to the app and sending several false notifications. After several random photos sent to the system without any visible licence plate
the system signs the assignments given by Fernand account as "possibly unsafe" and after agents checks that no violation is actually in any of 
the photos sent to the system his account gets blocked and Fernand can no longer interfere with the normal activities of authorities using
the app. After being blocked Fernand stops complaining about the app and a week later he finds a parking area to replace his former parking spot.
